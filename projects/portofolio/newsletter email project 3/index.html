Into the blue again

Click here to subscribe to this newsletter >

Last week, I heard from many of you who also have to drag yourself to exercise (I see you! hi!). It got me thinking, Why on earth did it make sense to me to talk about exercise in a newsletter about design and marketing? I was having these thoughts in kickboxing class when it hit me (hey, dopey pun!): The two share a balance between habituation and surprise. Your body needs a routine and a regularity with exercise to get in shape and stay there, to keep your mood and your body running smoothly. But if you do the same exercise over and over, you plateau. Your mind checks out and so does your body. The same is true of marketing: Your customers need to hear from you regularly, but you risk burning them out or boring them if you rely too heavily on the same message. What fixes this? Surprise! You surprise your body with new activities (I’m going to try krav maga next), and you surprise your customers with things they didn’t even know they wanted. That’s what we’ll be planning for next year--excellent surprises. Stay tuned.

Happy creating!

Jennifer Braunschweiger

Senior Director of Content Marketing
